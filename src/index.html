BIOGRAPHY

Based in Madrid, Alex Nogueira (27) is a Spanish composer, producer, and singer. 
After releasing four cover albums and directing three music videos, he first released an original single called in 2015, produced and written by himself.
In 2016 he co-wrote Burnin', the debut single by Kapchula, and submitted his second single titled to the national selection of the Eurovision Song Contest 2017. 

He also co-wrote the original soundtrack for the short-film titled ‘Güestía’. In 2020 he released a first EP of two called 'p rólogo'. In 2021 he released the second part titled 'epílogo'.

By the end of 2021, Alex Nogueira wrote the Carrefour Club jingle for the supermarket chain.
